Teddy is a second-year PhD student in the Oden Institute's Computational Science, Engineering, and Mathematics (CSEM) program. His research is focused on computational models of the heart and lungs to examine how disease affects both systems in tandem.

Teddy received his B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Operations Research from Rice University in 2024 and is thrilled to have made the short trip back to Austin to begin his research in the Predictive Modeling in Medicine Lab.
