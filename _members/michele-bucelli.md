Dr. Michele Bucelli is a Research Associate in the Predictive Modeling in Medicine Lab at the Center for Computational Medicine. His research deals with developing multiphysics mathematical and computational models of the cardiac function.

Dr. Bucelli obtained a PhD in 2023 at MOX, Mathematics Department, Politecnico di Milano, with a thesis on fully coupled multiphysics and multiscale simulation of the human heart. During his PhD and post-doc research he developed and consolidated a framework that enables the coupled simulation of the electrical, mechanical and hemodynamic processes from which the heartbeat arises.

At UT Austin, Dr. Bucelli plans on enhancing this framework towards clinical applications, integrating it in data acquisition and assimilation pipelines, to build towards patient-specific personalized models of the heart that can be used for diagnosis and treatment planning.
